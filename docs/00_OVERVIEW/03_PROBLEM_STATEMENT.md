# Problem Statement

# Current State

Organizations deploy expensive cybersecurity infrastructure.

Examples

- Splunk
- Sentinel
- Wazuh
- CrowdStrike
- Elastic
- Zeek
- Suricata

Despite these investments, organizations repeatedly investigate similar incidents.

---

# Why?

Knowledge disappears.

Current workflow

Alert

↓

Investigation

↓

Incident Report

↓

Archive

↓

Forgotten

---

# Pain Points

## Knowledge Loss

Analyst expertise leaves with employees.

---

## Repeated Investigations

Teams solve identical incidents multiple times.

---

## Detection Stagnation

Lessons are rarely reused.

---

## Long Response Time

Analysts spend hours searching historical reports.

---

## Fragmented Information

Evidence exists in multiple systems.

---

# Impact

Longer MTTR

Repeated analyst effort

Higher operational cost

Loss of organizational expertise

Poor incident consistency

---

# Solution

ODIN introduces Institutional Cyber Memory.

Instead of storing reports,

ODIN stores

- Knowledge
- Relationships
- Decisions
- Lessons
- Playbooks
- Evidence

Every future investigation benefits from previous investigations.
