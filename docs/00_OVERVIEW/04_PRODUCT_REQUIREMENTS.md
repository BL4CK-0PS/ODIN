# Product Requirements Document

# Product Name

ODIN

Operational Defense Intelligence Network

---

# Product Category

Institutional Cyber Memory

---

# Primary Objective

Reduce investigation time by enabling analysts to reuse previous investigations.

---

# Functional Requirements

FR-1

Upload incident logs.

---

FR-2

Reconstruct attack timeline.

---

FR-3

Extract entities.

---

FR-4

Map MITRE ATT&CK techniques.

---

FR-5

Store canonical incident.

---

FR-6

Search similar incidents.

---

FR-7

Explain similarity.

---

FR-8

Visualize investigation relationships.

---

# Non Functional Requirements

Response Time

<5 seconds

---

Scalability

100k+ investigations

---

Explainability

100%

Every recommendation must include evidence.

---

Availability

99.9%

---

Security

No incident data leaves organizational infrastructure.

---

# Success Criteria

Analyst uploads incident.

↓

ODIN reconstructs incident.

↓

ODIN finds similar investigations.

↓

ODIN recommends previous playbooks.

↓

Investigation time decreases.
