# Judge FAQ

---

Q

Why not use Splunk?

A

Splunk analyzes logs.

ODIN preserves investigative knowledge.

---

Q

Why Neo4j?

A

Relationships are first-class objects.

Graph traversal is natural for investigations.

---

Q

Why Qdrant?

A

Fast semantic retrieval.

Supports historical investigation search.

---

Q

Can this work without AI?

A

Yes.

Threat Memory uses deterministic matching first.

AI improves explanations.

---

Q

Can enterprises deploy this?

A

Yes.

Local-first architecture.

No cloud dependency.

---

Q

How do you prevent hallucinations?

A

AI never determines facts.

Only explains validated evidence.

---

Q

What happens when analysts leave?

A

Their investigation knowledge remains inside ODIN.

Institutional knowledge stays with the organization.
